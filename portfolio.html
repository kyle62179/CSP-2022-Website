Third Project(pltw 1.3.1). Christmas E-Card.

For my third PLTW project I worked with Colman on an interactive Christmas E-Card.



The recipient is given instructions for the card in the console. They can click Rudolf to Receive a short holiday message.
The user can also click on the Santa flying across in his sleigh. This will update a simple score counter after every click.

Fourth Project(Scratch). Soccer Game.

For my fourth project I worked with Colman on a Soccer Game with penalty kicks at the end.



This fun two player soccer game was created with Scratch. Simply press space to start the game and use arrow keys or wasd to move the characters.
Once either player reaches a score of 3 the screen will switch to the penalty kick setup and players will be
able to aim with the mouse and shoot with the up arrow or w keys.

Fifth Project(PLTW). Data Analysis.

For my fifth project I worked with Kam on a data analysis project.



We were tasked with trying to indentify which biome our rover on an alien planet is located. Our rover gave us a stream of data which was
collected from a gyroscope, sound, temperature and light sensor. We then organized and made graphs to visualize the data and
decided which sensors that data came from. Finally we compared the data collected to the environments on the alien planet and we belive that
our rover is in the desert plains.
